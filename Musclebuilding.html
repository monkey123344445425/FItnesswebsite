Train for aesthetics

Gym is one of the main components in Muscle Building. It is mainly used for looking good, bodybuilding or strength

Here are some steps that you take to build it.

  • Go to the gym atleast 4 times per week and the other days train for cardio and stretching
  • Do progressive overload as per your strength level increase
  • Eat 1.8-2.2 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight per day
  • Target for 7+ hours of quality sleep
  • Drink atleast 2 litres throughout the day
  • Consult an expert before taking supplementation
  • Be consistent for great results!!

    • Strength Training

  • Strength train is similar to aesthetic training
  • If you experience persistent pain or discomfort, back off and reassess your approach. Pushing through pain can lead to injury.
  • Train till failure and take breaks in between workouts
  • Train atleast 5 times a week
  • Progressive overload as per your strength level increase
  • Also focus on recovery a lot

    • How to train:

    Go to a gym or if you are doing home gym then order adjustable weight dumbbells

    Chest workouts

  • Barbell Bench Press: Warm-up: 1 set of 10 reps with light weight Working Sets: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Flyes: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Push-Ups (weighted if possible): 3 sets to failure (aim for at least 8-12 reps)
  • Cable or Machine Chest Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Chest Dips (if available): 3 sets to failure (use assistance if needed)
  • Chest Stretch: Finish with a deep chest stretch for 30 seconds to 1 minute to improve flexibility and aid recovery.

    • This is not an expert/doctor advice and not everything is mentioned. So please confront a gym trainer before doing something.
